ABOUT THE BRAND

Forbidden Planet is the UK’s leading retailer for science fiction, fantasy, comics and gaming merchandise.

This campaign focuses on boosting awareness and attendance for Friday gaming sessions by making events more visible, more structured, and easier to join.

For The Collectors

We sell merchandise from all the greatest films and TV shows including the Marvel Cinematic Universe, the DC universe, Star Wars, Doctor Who, Game Of Thrones, Star Trek, Stranger Things, Transformers and many more. Whether you’re looking for toys, figure, collectibles or accessories we have them all on offer for you.

For the Comic Book Fans

We’re the largest UK stockist of all the latest comics and graphic novels. We have all your favourite super-heroes and their villainous nemeses. From Marvel and DC to Image and Boom, we have all of the newest comics with more being delivered every week.

For The Book Lovers

We offer huge ranges of books on science fiction and science fact, as well as cult and pulp titles that you won’t find anywhere else. You’ll discover the complete range of your favourite authors all the way from Asimov to Zelazny in both science fiction and fantasy. Plus, we host many signings with the finest names in the genre and can offer stock that may be signed by Guillermo del Toro, Neil Gaiman, Cory Doctorow, Mike Mignola, Neal Stephenson, Dan Slott, Charles Stross and hundreds more – you can lose yourself in the choice. Our range of specialist books covers every cult genre from hard science fact to space and alternative cultures to fantasy, horror and cult entertainment. You’ll find the best in photographic and art books as well as documentary and behind-the-scenes information about the latest movies and TV shows.

For The Gamers

Discover our core rulebooks, supplements and fiction from the worlds of Dungeons and Dragons, H P Lovecraft and Magic The Gathering – plus merchandise from popular games such as Assassin’s Creed or Halo.
